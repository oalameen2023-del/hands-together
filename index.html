About Us

Hands Together is dedicated to creating positive change through education, community development, and sustainable initiatives. We believe in empowering individuals while building stronger, more sustainable communities.

Our Mission

We are committed to sustainable development and community empowerment through three key pillars: quality education, social services, and environmental sustainability. Our mission is to create lasting positive change that benefits current and future generations.

Making a Difference

Through our educational and development programs, we're empowering youth across the Middle East to build a brighter future. Our initiatives focus on providing quality education, fostering leadership skills, and creating sustainable opportunities for growth.

Education Program

This program focuses on providing quality education and essential skills for children and youth. We aim to empower the next generation and create a lasting positive impact in the community.

Community Development Program

This program aims to support local communities through development initiatives such as vocational training, small projects, and enhancing youth leadership and civic participation skills.
